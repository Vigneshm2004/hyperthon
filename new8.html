Explore Insurance Options for Health and Medical Needs

Government-Provided Insurance Facilities

  • Medicare: A government-funded health insurance program for citizens aged 65 and above, and for younger people with disabilities.
  • Medicaid: A joint federal-state program that provides health coverage to low-income individuals and families.
  • Affordable Care Act (ACA): A health insurance marketplace that offers affordable health plans to individuals and families.

Access to Insurance Facilities

To access these insurance facilities, patients can:

  • Visit the official government websites: www.healthcare.gov and www.medicaid.gov
  • Contact their state Medicaid office or local health department
  • Reach out to a licensed health insurance agent or broker

Benefits of Government-Provided Insurance Facilities

  • Financial Assistance: Government-provided insurance facilities offer financial assistance to individuals in need of medical care.
  • Access to Quality Care: These facilities provide access to quality medical care and services.
  • Peace of Mind: Government-provided insurance facilities offer peace of mind to individuals and families, knowing that they are protected in case of medical emergencies.

Related Information from the Government of India

  • National Health Portal: A comprehensive resource for health-related information and services provided by the Government of India.
  • Ayushman Bharat: A national health insurance program that provides financial protection to vulnerable families.

Insurance Options Provided by the Government

  • Employee's State Insurance (ESI) Scheme: A self-financing social security and health insurance scheme for workers in the organized sector.
  • Central Government Health Scheme (CGHS): A health insurance scheme for central government employees and their dependents.
