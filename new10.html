What is Polio?

Polio, or poliomyelitis, is a highly contagious viral infection that can lead to paralysis. It primarily affects children under the age of 5.

Prevention

The best way to prevent polio is through vaccination. The polio vaccine is safe and effective and is usually given in childhood.

Other preventive measures include:

  • Good hygiene practices
  • Safe drinking water
  • Awareness and education about the disease
