Diet Plans

1. Diabetic Diet

Focus on low glycemic index foods, whole grains, and lean proteins.

2. Heart Healthy Diet

Emphasize fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and healthy fats.

3. Gluten-Free Diet

Avoid gluten-containing foods like wheat, barley, and rye.

4. Mediterranean Diet

Focus on olive oil, fish, nuts, and plenty of fruits and vegetables.

5. Low-FODMAP Diet

Avoid high-FODMAP foods to help manage IBS symptoms.

6. Ketogenic Diet

High-fat, low-carbohydrate diet to promote weight loss.

7. DASH Diet

Designed to combat high blood pressure with fruits and vegetables.

8. Paleo Diet

Focus on whole foods, lean proteins, and avoid processed foods.

9. Vegan Diet

Plant-based diet that excludes all animal products.

10. Raw Food Diet

Emphasize raw fruits, vegetables, nuts, and seeds.

11. Anti-Inflammatory Diet

Focus on foods that reduce inflammation in the body.

12. Low-Carb Diet

Reduce carbohydrate intake to promote weight loss.

13. Intermittent Fasting

Cycles between periods of eating and fasting.

14. Zone Diet

Balance macronutrients to control insulin levels.

15. Flexitarian Diet

Primarily vegetarian but allows occasional meat and fish.

16. Whole30 Diet

30-day program to eliminate sugar, grains, dairy, and legumes.

17. Nutritarian Diet

Focus on nutrient-dense foods for optimal health.

18. Candida Diet

Avoid sugar and yeast to combat Candida overgrowth.

19. Low-Sodium Diet

Reduce sodium intake to manage blood pressure.

20. Anti-Candida Diet

Focus on eliminating foods that feed Candida yeast.
